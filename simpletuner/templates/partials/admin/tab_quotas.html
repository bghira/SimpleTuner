About Quotas

Limit spending and job submissions. Quotas can be global, per-level, or per-user. User-specific quotas take highest priority.

Quota Types:
  • COST_MONTHLY / COST_DAILY - USD limits
  • CONCURRENT_JOBS - Max simultaneous jobs
  • JOBS_PER_DAY / JOBS_PER_HOUR - Rate limits
Actions When Exceeded:
  • BLOCK - Reject the job
  • WARN - Allow with warning
  • REQUIRE_APPROVAL - Queue for approval
